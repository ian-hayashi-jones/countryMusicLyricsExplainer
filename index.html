An exploration of country music lyrics

Section 1
First, we're going to rank words that are most common in country lyrics. "Love", for example, appears 21 times for every 10,000 words. Among these few words, "love" is used in country more. But what matters for our analysis is usage in country compared to usage in all music lyrics. We want words unique to the genre: high frequency in country, low everywhere else.
Section 2
Now we'll compare country usage to other genres. We used lyrics from X songs (about Y million words) across all music genres available on Genius lyrics, except country. In this non-country dataset, words such as "love" are even more common: 71 times per 10,000 words (vs. 21 in hip hop).
Section 3
Now you can see all the words we measured. Dots farther to the right are more popular in country. Dots farther up are more popular in other genres.
